Game's history

The game begins with an internal dialogue from John, saying that Bran (his crippled brother) had left for the wall in order to
drive the walkers away from the area, as the Night King is after him. In addition some of the characters (Daenerys and Aria)
are trapped in some areas that we have to go through to get to the wall and, therefore, we will have to rescue them to help us save Bran.
After having advanced and rescued the two companions, we reached the last phase, on the wall, where we found Bran face to face with the Night King.
There, Bran reveals his true identity...
