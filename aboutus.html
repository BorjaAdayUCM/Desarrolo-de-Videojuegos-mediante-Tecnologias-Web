Project designed by Borja Aday Guadalupe Luis and Jorge María Martín for the subject DVI during 2020

We are a couple of students from the Complutense University of Madrid enrolled in computer engineering and in the DVI subject we decided to make a video game based on the world of Game of Thrones, since it is a serie that we really liked. Throughout the project we had some problems, but we managed to implement a large part of what we had in mind, however there is still a lot that can be improved, the only necessary thing is desire and time !!!
